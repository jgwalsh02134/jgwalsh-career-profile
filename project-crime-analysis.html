Interactive Crime Analysis: Albany, NY (2021-2024)

Date: March 21, 2025

This report analyzes crime trends in Albany, New York, from 2021 to 2024, using data obtained through a Freedom of Information Law (FOIL) request. Findings reveal a peak in total crimes in 2023, a significant increase in rape cases in 2024, and other key public safety trends.

The interactive chart below allows for a granular exploration of Part I Violent Crimes. Use the buttons to toggle categories and compare trends over the four-year period.
