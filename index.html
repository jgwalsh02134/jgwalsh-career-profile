Crime Analysis Report: Albany, NY (2021-2024)

Date: March 21, 2025

This report analyzes crime trends in Albany, New York, from 2021 to 2024, using data from a FOIL request to the Albany Police Department. The analysis covers total crimes, violent crime categories, neighborhood distribution, case resolution rates, and police response times. Key findings include a 2023 peak in total crimes, a rise in specific violent offenses in 2024, and high rates of unresolved cases in certain areas.
