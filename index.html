Featured Project

Crime Analysis Report: Albany, NY (2021-2024)

Date: March 21, 2025

This report analyzes crime trends in Albany, New York, from 2021 to 2024, using data obtained through a Freedom of Information Law (FOIL) request from the Albany Police Department. The analysis focuses on total crimes, violent crime categories, neighborhood distribution, case resolution rates, and police response times. Findings reveal a peak in total crimes in 2023, a significant increase in rape cases in 2024, concentrated crime in specific neighborhoods, a high rate of unresolved cases, and variable police response times.
